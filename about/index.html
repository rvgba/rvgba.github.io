Who I Am

About Me

PMO by day, designer by passion. Based in Jakarta, working at the intersection of operations, product, and visual communication.

I'm Ravi — an Economics graduate from Universitas Indonesia (Cum Laude, GPA 3.60) with 8+ years of experience working as a Project Management Officer in Indonesia's fintech and on-demand service industry. Currently I'm a Special Projects Manager at Gojek (PT GOTO Gojek Tokopedia), where I serve as PMO for the Sales Excellence team.

My work sits at the intersection of product, operations, and policy — I coordinate cross-functional teams to ship projects that make a real difference for merchants and the broader MSME ecosystem in Indonesia. I care deeply about building systems that last: clear documentation, smart SOPs, and decisions rooted in data.

Alongside my PMO career, I've maintained a lifelong passion for art and design. I did paid editorial work throughout university and have freelanced in visual design — creating publications, web mockups, and event branding — from 2014 to 2020. I specialize in creating visual experiences that feel meaningful and intentional, not just pretty.

Outside work: cats, hiking, model kits, custom keyboards, and spending too long picking the right typeface.
